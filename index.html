Wenzhou Dialect Speech to Mandarin Text Conversion

This project studies Wenzhou dialect speech to Mandarin text conversion (ASR, ST), and provides relevant information on data and models.

Dataset

we create a parallel dataset containing Wenzhou dialect speech and the corresponding Mandarin text.

Hours Utterances Label
Train 10.78 5037 Mandarin Text
Dev 1.86 1004 Mandarin Text
Test 5.19 2027 Mandarin Text

Data comes from A local news video program in Wenzhou dialect.

Due to copyright issues, we may not be able to make the complete dataset public.

Models

We release two models for the task of converting Wenzhou dialect speech to Mandarin text, based on TeleSpeech-ASR1.0-large and Wav2Vec2-XLS-R-300M.
